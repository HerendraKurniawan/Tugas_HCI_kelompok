About Us

Golden Circle is a famous jewelry store established in 1847.
With a lot of branches all over the world, Golden Circle always
consistent in providing only the high-quality jewelry.

Golden Circle is referred to as the home of World Class Jewelry,
especially their gold and white gold, which are supplied directly
from sightholders who sources gold from mines around the world.

Premium gold jewelry with the best quality materials
can only be found at the Golden Circle.
